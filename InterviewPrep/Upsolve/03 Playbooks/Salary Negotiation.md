# Playbook — Salary Negotiation

## Principles
- Never give a number first if avoidable; give a range anchored on research
- Negotiate total comp (base / equity / bonus / sign-on), not just base
- Get competing offers on the table
- Everything in writing before accepting

## Checklist
- [ ] Market data for level + location
- [ ] BATNA (walk-away) defined
- [ ] Ask for time to consider
- [ ] Counter once, specifically
